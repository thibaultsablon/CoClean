# CoClean

**Author**: Thibault SABLON<br>
**Video demo**: https://youtu.be/c_7aZgocABI
**Start date**: Jan 12, 2026<br>
**Submission date**: Jan 19, 2026

**Problem**: Removing corporate form suffixes on long lists of company names is time consuming and annoying.<br>
**Solution**: A simple web app to clean these names in one click.

**Input**: Raw list of company names.<br>
**Output**: Clean list of company names without corporate form suffix.

**How to run**:

1. Copy your list of company names from a spreadsheet column
2. Open index.html
3. Pate under "Dirty list:"
4. Click "Clean"
5. Click "Copy"
6. Paste on top of your original spreadsheet column
7. Done, or if you have another list click "Clear" and back to step 1

**Files**:

- app.js contains the logic
- index.htnl contains the layout
